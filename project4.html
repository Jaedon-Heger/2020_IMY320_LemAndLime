Neighbourhood

Project dates: Jul ‘19 - Sep ’19
Type: 3nd year individual project

Spec: Create a lyric video for any song. The lyric video should be presented via a aesthetically matching interface that should promote the album the song is from and create a mood matching that of the song.

For this project, the song Neighbourhood by American Athors was chosen. It is a nostalgic song that comments on the heartache that can come from missing the places one lived in the past and how those palces are linked to both good and bad memories.
