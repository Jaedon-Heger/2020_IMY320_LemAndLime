Fud

Project dates: Feb ‘20 - Jun ’20
Type: 3rd year group project

Spec: UI/UX research and user testing, used in an iterative design process. A shallow functional prototype of the app was designed in Adobe XD.

We created the Fud app with the intention of revolutionising the way people are able to find and share recipes for home cooking using their mobile devices.
